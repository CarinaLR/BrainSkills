The aim of BrainSkills is to help the student to get confident speaking in English helping them to reach their goals. Implementing many techniques of conversation, writing, and grammar. Each of which will hold an hour class, where the student will focus on their deficit by previous evaluation.

About us

Beginner

Use familiar everyday expressions and very basic phrases aimed at the satisfaction of needs of a concrete type. Can communicate in simple and routine tasks requiring a simple and direct exchange of information on familiar and routine matters.

Intermediate

Can deal with most situations likely to arise whilst travelling in an area where the language is spoken. Can produce simple connected text on topics which are familiar or of personal interest. Can interact with a degree of fluency and spontaneity that makes regular interaction with native speakers quite possible without strain for either party.

Advance

Can use language flexibly and effectively for social, academic and professional purposes. Can produce clear, well-structured, detailed text on complex subjects, showing a controlled use of organisational patterns.

Fluency

The class will be divided in conversation, writing, and grammar. Gaining vocabulary, pronunciation, grammar, reading comprehension, and writing. The student will summarise information from different spoken and written sources, reconstructing arguments and accounts in a coherent presentation.

On on one - online

In an hour class, according to the level of the student. The class will be divided in conversation, writing, and grammar. In this class, the student will learn the skills for speaking English. The class will focus on pronunciation, use of idioms, correct use of verbs, and nouns. All those techniques will help the student gain confidence and fluency once developing in English.

Groups 2 up to 6 - online

Focusing on grammar, writing, and conversation. The hour class will provide an average level according to the level of all participants. Gaining vocabulary, pronunciation, grammar, reading comprehension, and writing. Improving their English skills along with the group.

In Person

Supporting the needs of each person, providing linguistic information who is studying the language, knowing how you learn best and using those methods to study, increasing and expanding each need and understanding(speaking, listening, reading, and writing) working in a one to one, improving english skills you already have and the ones you want.
