Our services

BrainSkills offers custom online classes focusing on your weakness in the English language. Due to a previous evaluation, the instructors apply the best methodology for the student's needs.

On on one - online

In an hour class, according to the level of the student. The class will be divided in conversation, writing, and grammar. In this class, the student will learn the skills for speaking English. The class will focus on pronunciation, use of idioms, correct use of verbs, and nouns. All those techniques will help the student gain confidence and fluency once developing in English.

Groups 2 up to 6 - online

Focusing on grammar, writing, and conversation. The hour class will provide an average level according to the level of all participants. Gaining vocabulary, pronunciation, grammar, reading comprehension, and writing. Improving their English skills along with the group.

In Person

Supporting the needs of each person, providing linguistic information who is studying the language, knowing how you learn best and using those methods to study, increasing and expanding each need and understanding(speaking, listening, reading, and writing) working in a one to one, improving english skills you already have and the ones you want.
